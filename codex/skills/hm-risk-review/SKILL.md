# HM Risk Review

## Purpose

Protect the project from becoming a finance, gambling, game-approval, or harmful comparison product.

## Required Context

Read as needed:

- `docs/02-store-and-branding.md` for store positioning.
- `docs/08-risk-review.md` for current risk boundaries.
- `AGENTS.md` for product non-goals.

## Risk Checklist

Flag any use of:

- Real balance, credit limit, black-card application, loan, withdrawal, cashback, yield, investment, financial freedom plan, or "earn money".
- Top-percentile wealth comparisons or leaderboards.
- Recharge-for-cash mechanics.
- Lottery, gacha, slot, betting, mystery-box, probability reward, or jackpot language.
- UI that resembles a real bank, payment success page, statement, or credit product.
- Content that shames ordinary life or encourages real high consumption.

## Decision Levels

- `pass`: safe as written.
- `revise`: safe after specific wording or mechanic changes.
- `block`: do not ship this idea.

## Safer Alternatives

Replace finance-coded framing with:

- wish
- imagination
- virtual entertainment simulation
- share-card copy
- rich-person absurdity
- self-reward
