# HM Design Language

## Purpose

Make the product feel like a shareable emotional expression, not a fake luxury or finance app.

## Required Context

Read as needed:

- `docs/06-plan-design-review.md` for design conclusions.
- `docs/05-content-validation-plan.md` for sample content.
- `docs/08-risk-review.md` for forbidden visuals and words.

## Voice

Use:

- light comedy
- ordinary-life empathy
- specific contrast
- warm self-reward
- "friends can reply" phrasing

Avoid:

- fake luxury
- finance UI
- vulgar flexing
- status ranking
- generic motivational slogans

## Share Card Requirements

A share card should include:

- The user's original sentence.
- The generated result.
- One rich-person trouble or emotional punchline.
- A small "虚拟娱乐模拟" note.

It should not include:

- payment success
- bank-card replicas
- real balances
- loan or credit limit wording
- misleading asset statements
